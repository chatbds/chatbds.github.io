QUY TRNH 8 BC U T LT SNG CN H CHUNG C

1. Chn ch u t
Vic u tin l bn nn tm hiu ch u t bng cch ln google nh vo cm t Cc d n ca ch u t + tn bit ch u t hon thnh nhiu d n (c kinh nghim) hay l d n u tin (nhiu ri ro) v "Ch u t + tn + b" bit uy tn ca ch u t (c b bi trong nhng d n qua khng). Bn nn chn mua cn h ca ch u t c THNG HIU an ton vn, tnh thanh khon cao v d sinh li.
2. Xem d n tn mt
n xem tn mt d n tm hiu v tr c thun tin giao thng khng, c kt ni nhanh n cc khu vc trung tm ni v ngoi thnh khc hay khng, tin ch ni ngoi khu c nhng g, h tng c s hon chnh cha, hon thnh mng cha.

3. Xem lch thanh ton v ln chin lc thot hng

Xem lch thanh ton theo tin , cn i ti chnh, vch ra chin lc thanh ton v bn ra cht li. Bn c th bn cn h cht li vo 3 thi im chnh c th sinh li cao nht nh sau: trc khi ct nc (trc khi np 70%), sp bn giao cn h (trc khi np 95%) hoc trong vng 5 thng k t khi nhn bn giao cn h (sau khi np 95% + ph bo tr 2%). Tuy nhin, bn c th bn cn h cht li bt c khi no k t sau khi bn k hp ng mua bn vi ch u t nu li nhun t mc bn k vng.

4. Chin lc vay ngn hng u t lt sng cn h
Khi u t lt sng cn h chung c, bn nn vay ngn hng thay v tn dng ht vn hin c u t. Bn ch nn vay ti a l 50% gi tr cn h v tng gc + li vay phi tr hng thng khng vt qu 40% tng thu nhp ca bn. Bn cn m bo ngun thu nhp ca bn phi n nh, khng bp bnh. Nu ngn hng n hn n gc v li cho bn cho ti khi nhn cn h th qu tt. S cng tt hn nu ch u t u i tr li cho bn cho ti khi nhn cn h. Mt khc, v bn ch lt sng, tc u t ngn/ trung hn v s tt ton khon vay trc hn (vay t 3 thng 1,5 nm), bn nn chn gi vay c thi gian tr n cng di cng tt (20 25 nm) chia nh nht c th s tin gc v li np hng thng, gim nh p lc tr n cho bn.




5. Tnh chi ph u t v li nhun d kin

Bn phi tnh tng chi ph u t l bao nhiu. Tng chi ph u t bao gm:

Bn tm thy mt cn h B no cng phn khc v cng khu vc xy xong vi gi l 3,1 t ( c VAT v ph bo tr).
